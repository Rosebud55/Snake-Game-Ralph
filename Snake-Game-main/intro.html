Snake:

Revenge of the Apples!!!

In the heart of a lush orchard,

where the sweet fragrance of ripe fruit lingered in the air,

a silent rivalry brewed between two unlikely adversaries:

the cunning serpent the snake

and the unsuspecting denizens of the orchard.

For years, the snake

had prowled the verdant rows,

feasting upon the orchard's bounty with reckless abandon.

But little did he know, his gluttonous escapades had not gone unnoticed.

Beneath the emerald canopy,

whispers of discontent rustled among the trees,

as the fruits plotted their revenge.

One fateful dusk,

as the sun dipped below the horizon

and cast a golden glow upon the orchard,

the snake slithered forth,

his appetite stirred by the tantalizing scent of fresh fruit.

Unbeknownst to him,

the orchard's inhabitants had laid a clever trap,

hidden amidst the swaying branches and dappled shadows.

As the snake approached,

his forked tongue flickered eagerly,

anticipating his next meal.

But just as he reached out to claim his prize,

the orchard erupted into action.

One brave apple,

emboldened by the plight of his fallen comrades,

stepped forward to confront the snake.

With a defiant gleam in his eye

and a steely resolve in his core,

he prepared to face the serpent head-on.
